1. RecyclerView

1. Fragments
1. Fragment over Activity
1. SharedPreferences
1. CardView
1. CoordinatorLayout
1. SwipeRefreshLayout
1. Sorting data by collections
1. Unit testing
1. Instrumental testing
1. Animations
